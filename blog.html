What I Love

I’ve always loved the adrenaline of playing volleyball—the same rush I feel when riding my motorcycle on open roads, especially during a breathtaking sunset that paints the sky with colors of peace.

On quieter days, you’ll find me focused on a game of billiards. I started because of my brother, and now it's a passion I enjoy, whether casually or competitively.

I also join pageants, embracing both grace and passion, finding joy in both the spotlight and the community behind the stage.

But nothing beats those moments of simply having fun with my family, sharing laughter and stories over a warm cup of coffee. I really love coffee—especially when I feel sad and overwhelmed.

I cherish every adventure with my boyfriend, Dale—whether we’re exploring new places or just being silly together. And of course, I always look forward to spontaneous hangouts with my best friend, Karlyn, whose energy never fails to brighten my day.

Life feels complete when it’s a perfect mix of sports, simple joys, and being with the people I love—even if it’s just another night playing billiards and making memories.
